1.4 Describe the risks that might impact digital projects – what causes digital projects to fail? Why? The first thing that comes to my mind when thinking about what might cause digital project to fail is the project being too complex(often with the deadline being too close). Reason for that might be the project is too ambitious, team not skilled and/or experienced enough and there’s a risk of taking wrong or not the best (for that particular case) creation tools. The risk that might impact a digital project are running out of funds, not planned work good enough and making line of work very chaotic which might cause reaching milestones very hard, causing the low morale of the team.
Also, we live in a very fast developing world and at one point in the process of creation it might just happen that someone creates and publishes project like ours before us or at the same time, but which is just better.
